**Amazon** · Product Management

### [Project Sunroof 2.0: Atmospheric Correction](./02-PROJECT-SUNROOF-Variable-Correction-for-Improved-Efficiency/)

**Google** · PM × Applied Physics × ML

> *"The sun equation has a weak link. I fixed it with atmospheric physics."*

Corrects the fundamental Climatological Coefficient (Wc) in the solar energy equation by introducing two novel multipliers: the **Pressure Belt Multiplier (Pb)** based on global circulation cells and the **Marine Coastal Distance Correction (Mc)** using exponential decay modeling.

`E = (A · η · Isc) × [Wbase · Pb · Mc]`

### [Netflix: Sensory Personalization](./03-Netflix-Sensory-Personalization/)

**Netflix** · PM × Behavioural Psychology × Sensor Engineering

> *"Netflix knows what you watched. It doesn't know what you're feeling or what's your Vibe right now or how's weather around you?"*

Four context-aware features that extend personalization beyond the algorithm into the viewer's real world: **Gen-Pulse** (generational social proof), **Sense-Stream** (scene-synced haptic feedback), **Atmosphere** (weather-responsive UI), and **Pulse-Rec** (biometric mood sensing from wearables).

### [Bio-Azure Symbiosis: Data Center × Bio-Foundry Heat Integration](./04-BioAzure-DataCenter-BioFoundry-Symbiosis/)

**Microsoft** · PM × Industrial Symbiosis × Sustainability Engineering

> *"Everyone routes data center heat to homes. We route it to bio-foundries — because the thermal match is perfect, the load is 24/7, and the value is a platform, not a commodity."*

A complete implementation blueprint for co-locating precision fermentation bio-foundries with Microsoft data centers. DC waste heat at 45–50°C feeds directly into bioreactors needing 30–40°C — **no heat pump required** for 5 of 7 processes. Includes 7-dimension comparison vs district heating, process-level thermal mapping, financial model ($3–6M annual savings), phased PM roadmap, and regulatory analysis (Germany EnEfG mandates begin July 2026).
